☀️ OUR STORY

We built the antidote to doom-scrolling.

The Daily Cheer started with a simple idea: the world is full of genuinely wonderful things happening every single day — but you'd never know it from most news feeds.

Science breakthroughs. Communities showing up for each other. Animals doing hilarious things. Kids doing something incredible. Old people finishing what they started. Strangers being kind. These stories exist — they're just buried under the algorithm.

We dig them up every day and put them in one place. No ads disguised as content. No outrage bait. No "breaking news" that isn't. Just stuff that makes you feel better about being alive.

That's The Daily Cheer. We hope it becomes part of your morning.

Our Rules

  • ✅ Every story is real and verified before we publish it
  • ✅ No tragedy porn disguised as hope — uplifting means genuinely uplifting
  • ✅ Funny stories must actually be funny, not just weird
  • ✅ No political content — we're a vacation from all of that
  • ✅ Animals section is non-negotiable and will always exist
