Exciting News for Seniors: Free CBD Coming to Some Medicare Plans

May 15, 2026 · wire

Here's a little something to brighten your day if you or a loved one is on Medicare! The Trump administration has just greenlit a special test program that will allow certain Medicare patients to access CBD — yes, the calming, non-intoxicating compound found in cannabis — completely free of charge.

The idea behind this feel-good initiative is beautifully simple: researchers want to see whether CBD can help older adults manage certain symptoms and feel better day to day. If it works, it could also help reduce overall healthcare costs, meaning more savings for everyone down the road.

While the program is still in its early stages, it represents a genuinely hopeful step toward exploring new, natural ways to support the health and comfort of our beloved seniors. Stay tuned — this is one story we'll be watching with a big smile!
