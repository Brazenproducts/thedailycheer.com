How the Sun Brings Seasons to Earth, Mars, and Beyond

May 15, 2026 · wire

Have you ever wondered why we get cozy winters and sunny summers? It all comes down to our incredible Sun and the way planets tilt and travel around it. Every world in our solar system has its own unique relationship with that big, beautiful star at the center of it all!

Here on Earth, our seasons are a familiar rhythm we plan our lives around — pumpkin patches in fall, beach days in summer. But did you know Mars has seasons too? Because Mars takes nearly twice as long to orbit the Sun, its seasons stretch out much longer than ours. Imagine a winter that lasts twice as long!

Learning how the Sun shapes life across different worlds reminds us just how connected everything is. Whether you're enjoying a spring breeze or watching snow fall, the same star is looking after us all. How wonderful is that?
