York's Beloved Assembly Rooms Is Getting the Makeover It Deserves

May 14, 2026 · BBC Science

Great news for history lovers and York locals alike — one of the city's most treasured landmarks is about to get some well-earned TLC! The Assembly Rooms, a stunning building that has stood proudly in York for centuries, is set to undergo important repair work to keep it in tip-top shape for generations to come.

The York Conservation Trust has announced that the beautiful old building needs some attention to its roof and front portico — the grand pillared entrance that makes it such an iconic sight. Rather than letting time take its toll, the Trust is stepping in to make sure this architectural gem stays strong and safe.

It's wonderful to see people rallying around the places that make our communities special. Thanks to this dedicated care, visitors and locals will be able to enjoy the Assembly Rooms' magnificent character for many years ahead. Here's to preserving the best of the past!
