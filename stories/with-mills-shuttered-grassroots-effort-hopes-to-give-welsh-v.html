Wales Is Turning Old Mill Country Into a Breathtaking 83-Mile Trail

May 14, 2026 · Good News Network

Something really special is happening in the Welsh valleys, and we are absolutely here for it. A passionate group of locals has come together with a beautiful vision: transforming a historic mill region into a stunning 83-mile hiking trail that everyone can enjoy for generations to come.

The trail winds through landscapes that were once the beating heart of Wales's industrial past. Rather than letting those spaces sit quietly forgotten, this grassroots community effort is breathing fresh new life into the area — honoring its heritage while opening the door to outdoor adventure, tourism, and local pride.

What makes this story so warm and wonderful is that it started with ordinary people who simply cared deeply about their home. No big budget, no government mandate — just neighbors with a dream and the determination to make it real. Lace up your boots, Wales. Something wonderful is coming.
