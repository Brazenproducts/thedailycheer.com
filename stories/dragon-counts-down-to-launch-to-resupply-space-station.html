Dragon Gets Ready to Deliver Goodies to Space Station Crew

May 15, 2026 · wire

Get ready for a little cosmic excitement, friends! A SpaceX Dragon spacecraft is counting down to liftoff, packed full of supplies for the wonderful astronauts living and working aboard the International Space Station. It's basically the world's most amazing care package, and it's about to take the ride of a lifetime.

From fresh provisions to scientific experiments and a few thoughtful surprises from home, Dragon's cargo helps the station crew keep doing the incredible research that benefits all of us back on Earth. Every successful resupply mission is a beautiful reminder of just how much teamwork it takes to keep humans thriving among the stars.

So when that countdown hits zero and Dragon roars skyward, take a moment to smile and cheer along. It's a wonderful day for science, exploration, and the boundless human spirit — and the best part? The adventure is only just beginning!
