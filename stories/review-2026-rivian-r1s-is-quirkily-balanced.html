The 2026 Rivian R1S Electric SUV Is Here to Delight You

May 16, 2026 · wire

If you've ever looked at Rivian's R1T pickup truck and thought, "I wish that came as a family SUV," well — great news! The 2026 Rivian R1S is essentially exactly that, and honestly? That's a wonderful thing.

The R1S brings all the rugged charm and electric personality of the R1T and wraps it up in a roomy, adventure-ready SUV package. It's the kind of vehicle that makes you want to pack your bags, load up the family, and head somewhere beautiful — all without stopping at a gas station.

There's something genuinely exciting about a car that feels this thoughtfully put together. Rivian has crafted a ride that's full of character, surprisingly fun, and ready for wherever life takes you. Whether you're conquering mountain trails or school pickup duty, the R1S makes every trip feel like a little adventure worth celebrating.
