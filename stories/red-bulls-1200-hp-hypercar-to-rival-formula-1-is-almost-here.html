Red Bull's jaw-dropping hypercar is almost here and it's wild

May 16, 2026 · wire

Remember when Red Bull teased us with that sleek, low-slung hypercar called the RB17? Well, buckle up—it's finally becoming real. After nearly two years of anticipation, this beauty is set to hit the road in 2026, and it's shaping up to be something truly special.

What makes the RB17 so exciting? This machine is engineered to generate nearly twice its own weight in downforce, which basically means it'll hug the road like nothing you've ever seen. It's the kind of engineering marvel that makes car lovers' hearts skip a beat—a hypercar that brings Formula 1 technology straight to the street.

The countdown is officially on, and 2026 is closer than you might think. For anyone who dreams about pushing the limits of what's possible on four wheels, the RB17 represents the future of driving. It's a reminder that sometimes the most incredible innovations come from people bold enough to dream big and build even bigger.
