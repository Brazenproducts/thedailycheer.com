Red Bull's Incredible Dream Hypercar Is Finally Almost Ready to Roll

📹 Hear the Red Bull RB17 scream at 15,000rpm — Red Bull / YouTube

May 16, 2026 · wire

Something truly exciting is on the horizon for car lovers everywhere! Red Bull, the brand best known for its championship Formula 1 racing team, has been quietly building what might be the most thrilling road car ever created — and it's almost ready to meet the world.

The car is called the RB17, and it's genuinely jaw-dropping. Imagine a vehicle so aerodynamically clever that it pushes itself into the road with nearly twice its own weight in force — basically giving it superhero-level grip. It has a whopping 1,200 horsepower under the hood, borrowing real magic straight from the world of Formula 1 racing.

After two years of anticipation since Red Bull first teased this beauty, 2026 is shaping up to be the year dreams become reality. For anyone who's ever watched a race and thought 'I want a piece of that' — your moment is almost here!
