Red Bull's Breathtaking Dream Hypercar Is Nearly Ready to Roll

May 16, 2026 · wire

Something truly exciting is coming for car lovers everywhere! Red Bull has been quietly working on one of the most extraordinary road cars ever built — the RB17 — and after nearly two years of anticipation, 2026 is shaping up to be the year it finally arrives.

So what makes this car so special? Imagine a vehicle so aerodynamically brilliant that it essentially gets glued to the road with a force nearly double its own weight. With a jaw-dropping 1,200 horsepower under the hood, the RB17 draws its inspiration straight from the world of Formula 1 racing — bringing that heart-pounding performance to an actual road car.

For anyone who has ever dreamed of experiencing the magic of motorsport up close, this is a genuine dream come true. The future of driving is looking incredibly bright, and we honestly cannot wait to see this beauty in action!
