Shanghai Recycles a Stunning 98% of Its Industrial Waste

May 13, 2026 · Good News Network

Here's a story that proves big cities can make big changes when they put their hearts into it! Shanghai, one of the busiest metropolises on the planet, just hit an incredible milestone: a whopping 98% recycling rate for its industrial waste. That's nearly every last scrap finding a second life instead of ending up forgotten in a landfill.

It all began back in 2019, when the city rolled out an ambitious waste management plan. Residents, businesses, and local leaders came together to sort, separate, and rethink the way they handle everyday materials. What started as a learning curve quickly blossomed into a citywide habit, with neighborhoods cheering each other on every step of the way.

Now, just a few years later, Shanghai is showing the world what teamwork and determination can accomplish. It's a beautiful reminder that small daily choices, multiplied across millions of people, can spark truly extraordinary change!
