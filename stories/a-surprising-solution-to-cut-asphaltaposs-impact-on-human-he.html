This Scientist Found a Brilliant Way to Make Our Roads Healthier

May 16, 2026 · wire

Here's something that might surprise you: the roads beneath our feet could be affecting the air we breathe. Researcher Elham Fini from Arizona State University has spent years looking into how asphalt — the stuff that paves our streets and driveways — releases emissions that can impact our health. And the good news? She's found some genuinely exciting ways to tackle it.

Professor Fini, who specializes in sustainable engineering, has been working on eco-friendly alternatives that could reduce those harmful emissions significantly. Her approach looks at smarter, greener materials that can be used in everyday road construction without turning the whole system upside down.

What we love most about this story is that someone is out there quietly making our world safer, one road at a time. Thanks to dedicated scientists like Professor Fini, the path forward — literally — is looking cleaner, healthier, and brighter for all of us!
