Chef Takes Amazing Arctic Adventure to Understand Healthy Eating

May 15, 2026 · wire

Talk about commitment to the craft! British chef Mike Keen is about to embark on the ultimate culinary expedition across Greenland's stunning icy landscape. For an entire month, he'll be skiing through this breathtaking terrain while eating only traditional fermented seal—a dietary staple of the Inuit people for thousands of years. It's not your typical cooking show, but it's absolutely fascinating!

What makes this journey extra special is that Mike isn't just doing this for the adventure (though how cool is that?). Researchers will be studying how this ancient diet affects his gut health and overall wellness. By exploring the science behind traditional Inuit eating patterns, scientists hope to uncover insights that could help all of us feel our best.

Mike's expedition is a beautiful reminder that there's so much we can learn from indigenous wisdom and traditional foods. By diving deep into these time-tested practices, we're not only honoring cultural heritage—we're discovering new ways to nourish our bodies. Now that's an adventure worth celebrating!
