Baby Eagles Jackie and Shadow Are Learning to Fly and We're Obsessed

Photo: Popular Science / Friends of Big Bear Valley

📹 Video: Friends of Big Bear Valley / YouTube — Sandy opens his full wings — is he ready to fly?

May 16, 2026 · wire

Get ready to smile, because Jackie and Shadow's adorable eaglets are growing up fast! The fluffy little ones have been spotted practicing their stomping and flapping, and honestly, it's the cutest thing you'll see all week.

These tiny wing workouts are a huge deal in the eagle world. Every stomp and flap builds the muscles these babies will need to soar through the sky one day. It's like their version of going to the gym, except infinitely more charming and covered in soft feathers.

Watching these eaglets take their first steps toward independence is a beautiful reminder that big journeys always start with small, wobbly beginnings. Before we know it, Jackie and Shadow's little ones will be spreading their wings and taking flight — and we'll be cheering them on every single moment of the way!
