Beloved British Motorcycle Brand Norton Is Gloriously Back and Better Than Ever

May 17, 2026 · wire

Great news for anyone who loves a good comeback story — Norton Motorcycles is roaring back to life! The iconic British brand, beloved for decades by riders and dreamers alike, has just unveiled a stunning new superbike packed with a thrilling 206-horsepower V4 engine. And yes, it is every bit as exciting as it sounds.

Norton has deep roots in motorcycle history, with a legacy tied to legendary races and the kind of cool that never really goes out of style. Seeing this storied name return with such an ambitious, powerful machine feels like watching an old friend walk back through the door — and this time, they brought something spectacular.

What makes this launch even sweeter is the surprisingly accessible pricing, which means more riders can dream of getting their hands on one. Here's to Norton's bright new chapter — the road ahead looks absolutely wonderful!
