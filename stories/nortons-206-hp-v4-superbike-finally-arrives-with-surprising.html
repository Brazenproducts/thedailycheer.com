Norton Motorcycles Is Back and Its New Bike Is Stunning

May 17, 2026 · wire

Great news for motorcycle lovers everywhere — Norton Motorcycles has officially returned, and it brought something truly special along for the ride. The iconic British brand has unveiled a brand-new superbike powered by a thrilling 206-horsepower V4 engine, and fans could not be more excited to see this legend back in action.

Norton has a history that makes motorcycle enthusiasts absolutely glow. For decades, the name meant speed, style, and a certain cool British spirit that captured hearts around the world. After a long absence, the company worked hard to rebuild itself from the ground up, and that dedication is clearly paying off in a big way.

What really has people buzzing is the surprisingly accessible pricing on this new machine. Norton seems genuinely committed to welcoming riders back into the fold. It's a beautiful reminder that some of the best comebacks are absolutely worth the wait!
