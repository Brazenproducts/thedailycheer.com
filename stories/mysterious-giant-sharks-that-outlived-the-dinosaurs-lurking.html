Ancient Sharks Still Thriving in Puget Sound Today

May 17, 2026 · wire

Here's something absolutely fascinating: swimming in the waters of Puget Sound are some of the ocean's most incredible living fossils! Sixgill sharks, those mysterious deep-sea giants, have been cruising Earth's oceans since before dinosaurs roamed the planet. Can you imagine? These remarkable creatures watched the dinosaurs come and go, and they're still here today, quietly going about their business in our own backyard.

These ancient sharks are like time travelers, perfectly adapted survivors that have found their happy home in the cold, deep waters of Puget Sound. Scientists are absolutely thrilled to study them right here in the Pacific Northwest, learning secrets about evolution and marine life that help us understand our planet better. It's like having a living window into prehistoric times!

What makes this even more wonderful is that people are increasingly interested in protecting these amazing animals and their habitat. By learning about sixgill sharks and other deep-sea creatures, we're discovering just how precious and interconnected our oceans truly are. Nature really does keep the most incredible surprises right in our own waters!
