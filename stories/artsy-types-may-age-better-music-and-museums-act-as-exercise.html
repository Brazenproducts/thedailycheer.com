Turns Out Visiting Museums and Humming Along Could Help You Age Beautifully

May 16, 2026 · Good News Network

Here's a wonderful excuse to finally book those concert tickets or spend a lazy Sunday wandering through your favorite art gallery — science says it might actually be keeping you younger! A new study has found that people who regularly engage with the arts age about 4% more slowly than those who don't. That's right, soaking up a little culture counts as a workout for your body and mind!

Researchers discovered that activities like listening to live music, visiting museums, or attending theater performances have a measurable effect on how we age. It seems that feeding your soul with creativity and beauty does far more than just lift your spirits in the moment.

So whether you love jazz clubs, pottery classes, or simply browsing your local gallery, know that every moment spent appreciating art is a little gift to your future self. How's that for a beautiful reason to get out and enjoy life?
