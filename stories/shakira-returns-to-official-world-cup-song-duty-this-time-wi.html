Shakira and Burna Boy Team Up for an Unforgettable World Cup Anthem

May 15, 2026 · wire

Get ready to have a brand new song stuck in your head, because the 2026 World Cup just got its official anthem! Shakira and the incredibly talented Burna Boy have joined forces to bring us "Dai Dai," and honestly, the combination of these two global superstars feels like a match made in music heaven.

This isn't Shakira's first time bringing the magic to a World Cup stage — she's done it before and absolutely won our hearts. Teaming up with Afrobeats sensation Burna Boy feels like a beautiful celebration of how music truly connects people from every corner of the world.

The best World Cup songs have a special way of living on long after the final whistle blows, becoming the soundtrack to some of our most treasured memories. Here's hoping "Dai Dai" becomes one of those songs that makes us smile for decades to come!
