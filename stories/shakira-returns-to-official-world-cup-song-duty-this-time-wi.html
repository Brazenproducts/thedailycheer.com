Shakira and Burna Boy Are Bringing the World Cup Vibes

📹 Shakira's official World Cup 2026 anthem — YouTube

May 15, 2026 · wire

Get ready to have a new song stuck in your head for the next year — in the best possible way! FIFA has officially announced that Shakira and Burna Boy are teaming up for the 2026 World Cup anthem, titled Dai Dai. And honestly? We could not be more excited about this dream team.

Shakira is no stranger to this kind of magic. She's helped soundtrack the beautiful game before, and she clearly knows how to make the whole world move. Paired with Burna Boy's irresistible Afrobeats energy, this collaboration feels like a celebration waiting to happen.

The best World Cup songs have a funny way of outlasting the tournament itself — they become the soundtrack to cherished memories for millions of fans everywhere. Dai Dai has every ingredient to be one of those timeless feel-good anthems. Start warming up those dance moves now!
