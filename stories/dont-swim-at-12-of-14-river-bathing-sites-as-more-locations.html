England Is Getting Serious About Cleaner Rivers and We Love It

May 15, 2026 · BBC Science

Big things are happening for England's rivers! Authorities have been closely monitoring 14 official river bathing spots across the country, and while some currently have high bacteria levels that make swimming a no-go for now, the really exciting news is that these sites are being watched, measured, and cared for like never before.

Think of it this way — you can only fix what you can see. By officially designating these river bathing sites and testing them regularly, scientists and local leaders now have the real data they need to push for meaningful improvements. More locations are even being added to the list, which means more rivers getting the attention they deserve.

Clean, swimmable rivers have been a dream for communities across England for generations, and this careful monitoring is a huge step toward making that dream a reality. The best days on these riverbanks are still ahead!
