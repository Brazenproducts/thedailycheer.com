This Clever Bike Light Is About to Make Your Rides So Much Safer

May 15, 2026 · wire

If you've ever hopped on your bicycle and wondered whether drivers could actually see you back there, we have some genuinely exciting news for you. Meet the Hi-Tail — a tall, telescoping safety beacon that's about to become your new favorite riding companion.

Unlike traditional tail lights that can get lost in the visual noise of busy streets, the Hi-Tail rises up to catch eyes from a real distance. It's thoughtfully designed to make sure you stand out, whether you're cruising through the neighborhood at dusk or navigating a busy evening commute.

At the end of the day, feeling safe on your bike means you can focus on what really matters — the joy of the ride itself. With a little light like this watching your back, you can pedal forward with confidence and a big smile on your face. Happy riding, friends!
