Renault Brings Joy Back to Electric Cars With Convertible Surprise

May 17, 2026 · wire

Just when it seemed like every electric car was starting to look the same, Renault had a delightful idea: why not make driving fun again? Say hello to the new open-top Renault 4 E-Tech, a charming convertible that takes us back to simpler times while zooming us into the future. With its retro-inspired design and that gorgeous folding fabric roof, this little beauty proves that going electric doesn't mean sacrificing personality.

That gorgeous roof? It's not just for show. Imagine cruising through summer with the sun on your face and the wind in your hair—all while being kind to the planet. The convertible top folds away beautifully, transforming your daily commute into a mini-vacation whenever you want. It's the kind of detail that reminds us cars can be more than just transportation; they can bring genuine joy to our lives.

In a world where everything starts to feel a bit cookie-cutter, Renault's convertible EV is a breath of fresh air. It shows that innovation and nostalgia can dance together perfectly, creating something truly special. Now that's something worth celebrating!
