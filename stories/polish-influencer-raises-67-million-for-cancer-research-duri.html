Polish Influencer Streams for 9 Days Straight and Raises Millions for Cancer Research

May 15, 2026 · Good News Network

Sometimes one person really can change the world — and a Polish influencer just proved it in the most heartwarming way possible. Over the course of nine incredible days, they stayed live on camera, and their community showed up in a absolutely massive way.

By the time the stream wrapped up, viewers had donated a jaw-dropping $67 million for cancer research. That's not a typo! Fans from around the globe tuned in, cheered each other on, and gave generously — turning a single livestream into one of the most remarkable fundraising moments the internet has ever seen.

This story is a beautiful reminder of what people can accomplish when they come together around something that matters. Science gets a huge boost, families battling cancer get a reason to hope, and the rest of us get a little more faith in humanity. We love to see it!
