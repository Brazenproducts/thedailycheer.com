Special Delivery! SpaceX Sends Science and Smiles to Space Station

May 15, 2026 · wire

Get ready to cheer, space fans! NASA and SpaceX have teamed up once again for a truly out-of-this-world delivery — the 34th resupply mission to the International Space Station. Packed with fresh science experiments, helpful gear, and goodies for the hardworking crew, this rocket lifted off carrying the hopes and dreams of researchers right here on Earth.

Think of it as the world's most exciting care package! From cutting-edge studies that could shape the future of medicine to clever new tech being tested in zero gravity, every item onboard has a story to tell. Our astronauts orbiting high above will soon unpack these treasures and get to work making discoveries that benefit everyone back home.

It's a beautiful reminder of what we can achieve when curiosity and teamwork take flight together. Here's to brilliant minds, bold missions, and a future that just keeps getting brighter. Onward and upward!
