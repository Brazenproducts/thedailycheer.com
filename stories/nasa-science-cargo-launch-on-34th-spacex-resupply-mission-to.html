SpaceX Just Delivered a Trunk Full of Wonder to Space Station

May 15, 2026 · wire

Great news from the cosmos! SpaceX successfully launched its 34th resupply mission to the International Space Station, sending a fantastic haul of science experiments, fresh supplies, and cargo to the astronauts living and working up there among the stars.

Think of it like the world's most extraordinary delivery run — rocketing everything our brilliant space crew needs to keep doing their incredible work hundreds of miles above Earth. From cutting-edge scientific research to everyday essentials, this mission has got it all covered.

It's a beautiful reminder of what humans can accomplish when we dare to dream big and work together. Every single launch brings us closer to discoveries that could change life here on Earth for the better. Here's to the dedicated teams at NASA and SpaceX who make the seemingly impossible look almost routine — you're all absolutely stellar!
